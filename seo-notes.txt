SEO Notes - Missing HK Search Terms
=====================================

These are search terms HK clients would use on Google.
Try to include them naturally in your Chinese (hk.js) content:

1. 線上心理治療香港 (online psychotherapy Hong Kong)
2. 香港心理輔導師 (Hong Kong counsellor)
3. 廣東話治療 (Cantonese-speaking therapy)
4. 網上輔導 (online counselling)
5. 創傷輔導 (trauma counselling)
6. 港人心理健康 (Hong Kong people mental health)
7. 網上治療 (online therapy)

Content Issues to Fix
=====================

1. Memories page - not translated to Chinese at all
2. Several images missing alt text (therapy page, memories page, session info page)
3. Section headings use <p> tags instead of <h2>/<h3> - Google can't see your page structure
4. "Social Dreaming" is never translated - consider adding 集體夢境 or 夢境工作坊
